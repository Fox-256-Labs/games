About This Visualization

This line chart race shows the temperature changes in NYC over a full year.I visited NYC in the summer of 2016 and was always curious what the weather was like the rest of the year. It was such a hot and humid summer when I visited.

Key Insights

  • Summers are hot and humid
  • Winter in NYC is usually cold and tends to snow often
